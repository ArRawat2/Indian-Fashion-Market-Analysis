# Indian Fashion Market Trend Analysis (2025)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the Indian fashion retail market to identify trends and provide business insights for Winter 2024–25 and Summer 2025 product launches. The focus is on mass-market brands such as Max, Pantaloons, Biba, H&M India, etc.

The goal is to understand seasonal demand, category performance, color preferences, pricing behavior, and customer segments using data analysis and visualization.

## Objective

- Identify top-performing categories by season
- Analyze color preferences in the Indian market
- Understand price sensitivity and revenue-driving price ranges
- Study customer demographics and city-wise performance
- Support product launch, pricing, and inventory decisions

## Dataset

- Size: ~50,000 rows
- Data includes:
  - Sales transactions
  - Product details
  - Customer demographics
  - Competitor pricing
  - Search trends

## Tools Used

- Excel (Power Query) – Initial data cleaning and validation
- Python (Pandas, NumPy) – Data transformation, feature creation, and EDA
- Power BI – Interactive dashboard and business visualization

## Key Steps

1. Cleaned and standardized raw data using Excel and Power Query
2. Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) using Pandas
3. Created derived features such as season, price buckets, and revenue metrics
4. Built a multi-page Power BI dashboard for business insights

## Dashboard Pages

1. Executive Sales Overview – High-level KPIs and seasonality
2. Seasonal Category Performance – Category-wise revenue by season
3. Color & Pricing Strategy – Top colors and price bucket analysis
4. Customer & Market Insights – Age group, gender, and city analysis

## Key Insights

- Winter wear (Jackets, Thermals, Shawls) is the highest revenue contributor
- Neutral colors (Beige, Black, Navy, Olive) dominate sales
- ₹3000–₹4999 price range generates the highest revenue
- Core customers are in the 19–35 age group
- Metro cities contribute the majority of revenue

## Outcome

The project provides data-driven recommendations for seasonal product launches, pricing strategy, and inventory planning in the Indian fashion market.
